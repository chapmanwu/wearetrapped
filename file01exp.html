Earth Is A Prison Planet

File 01 explanation/human skin/

----------------------------

Human skin is the clothes of prisoner
Humans’ reaction to sunlight, the very thing we need to sustain life, is out of synch with most other animals on the planet. Many, for example, can have exposure to sun all day, every day, for long periods of time. For humans, that isn’t the case. Not only will we burn in the sun in a matter of hours, but long-term exposure often results in skin-cancers. Furthermore, we squint our eyes in the sun, again, something that appears to be unique to humans.
